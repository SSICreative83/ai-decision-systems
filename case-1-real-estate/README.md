# AI-Driven Commercial Real Estate Portfolio Risk Analytics  
### Deep Learning + Graph Neural Networks + LLM Integration

## Overview
Traditional commercial real estate (CRE) analytics evaluates assets independently.

However, real estate portfolios are inherently interconnected through:
- Geography  
- Economic conditions  
- Borrower relationships  
- Industry exposure  

This project introduces an AI-driven system that models CRE portfolios as **connected networks**, enabling more accurate and forward-looking risk assessment.

---

## Business Problem
CRE portfolio risk is influenced by multiple factors:

- Property financial performance (NOI, occupancy, valuation)  
- Macroeconomic conditions (interest rates, unemployment)  
- Market dynamics (rent trends, vacancy rates)  
- Hidden dependencies across assets  

Traditional methods fail to capture:
- Risk propagation across properties  
- Interdependencies between borrowers and markets  

---

## Solution

This system integrates three core AI components:

---

### 1. Deep Learning (Risk Prediction)

A neural network model predicts:

- Loan default probability  
- Property-level risk score  

Using:
- Property financial features  
- Loan characteristics  
- Macroeconomic indicators  
- Market trends  

---

### 2. Graph Neural Network (Portfolio Connectivity)

A Graph Neural Network (GNN) models relationships across:

- Properties  
- Borrowers  
- Geographic regions  

Key capabilities:

- Identifies **risk clusters** within portfolios  
- Captures **risk propagation across connected assets**  
- Models spatial and economic dependencies  

> Unlike traditional models, this approach treats CRE portfolios as **systems, not isolated assets**.

---

### 3. LLM-Based Market Intelligence

A Large Language Model (LLM) integrates unstructured data sources:

- Earnings call transcripts  
- News and market reports  

Output:

- Structured market risk summaries  
- Early signals of sector-level risk  

---

## System Architecture

- **Data Layer:** Property, loan, macroeconomic, and market data  
- **Model Layer:** Deep Learning + Graph Neural Network  
- **Intelligence Layer:** LLM-based text analysis  
- **Visualization Layer:** Portfolio risk dashboard  

---

## Example Outputs

- Property-level risk scores  
- Portfolio risk distribution  
- Geographic risk heatmaps  
- Risk cluster identification  
- LLM-generated market summaries  

*(Note: Demonstration based on synthetic data)*

---

## Key Insights

- Risk emerges from **interconnected systems**, not isolated assets  
- Geographic and economic relationships drive **hidden dependencies**  
- Market signals extracted from text data can provide **early warnings**  

---

## Impact

This system demonstrates how AI can transform CRE analytics:

- Improved visibility into portfolio-level risk  
- Early detection of emerging vulnerabilities  
- Enhanced decision support for risk management and investment strategy
